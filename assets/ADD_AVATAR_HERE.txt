IMPORTANT: Add your avatar image here!

1. Save your professional photo as "avatar.jpg" in this assets folder
2. The image should be:
   - At least 400x400px for good quality
   - Square or portrait orientation works best
   - JPEG format recommended for smaller file size

The portfolio is already configured to display this image:
- Desktop: Shows in the About section (4:5 portrait ratio)
- Mobile: Shows as a circular avatar in the Hero section

If no image is found, a placeholder will be shown instead.
